Contribute

Both technical and non-technical contributions are always welcome!

If you have any questions, feel free to drop by to chat.

Below are the areas you can help with:

Burninations and other tag-related moderation

If you're interested in helping with burninations, or other aspects of tag moderation, or possibly just moderation in general, we're always in need of more help on the frontlines.

If you're looking for an introduction to contributing to tag moderation, see the introduction. You can also drop by chat.

If you're looking to contribute to moderation in general, you may be interested in one or more of the related initiatives listed below.

Website

The website is built with Hugo 0.101.0

Related Initiatives

  • Charcoal

    Stack Exchange network group of volunteers with the goal of detecting and effectively removing spam from the network.

    SmokeDetector – a bot for detecting and reporting spam posts.

  • SOBotics

    Stack Exchange network-wide user organization for creating community governance bots, and more.

    Revenant – a bot for tracking zombie tags arisen after burnination.

    Rodgort – a project for managing burnination, synonym, and merge requests on Stack Overflow.

  • SOCVR

    Stack Overflow-based group of curators focusing on coordinated question closure, reopening, reviewing, and clean-up.

    Show Burninated Tags – indicates that a tag has been burninated on NATO pages.
